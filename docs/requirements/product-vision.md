# TicketFlow - Product Vision

## Overview

TicketFlow is a role-based client ticket management system that enables organizations to efficiently manage customer support requests through a structured ticket lifecycle.

The system allows clients to raise support tickets, support agents to resolve issues, and administrators to manage users, assignments, and overall system operations.

## Objectives

- Reduce manual ticket handling.
- Track every customer issue from creation to resolution.
- Maintain complete communication history.
- Provide visibility through dashboards and reporting.
- Ensure secure, role-based access to all resources.
